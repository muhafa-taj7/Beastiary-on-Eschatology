A Book of Wonders Concerning the Beast of the Resurrection

كِتَابُ عَجَائِبَ فِي دَابَّةِ الْقِيَامَةِ

Mustafa Tajammul

Know, O seeker, that the Day of Judgement is the end of times — and before it come signs to warn the people. Among the greatest of these signs are the beasts herein described, whose natures, appearances, and significance have been preserved.

✦

Index of Creatures

  • I. The Mahdī — The Rightly Guided One الْمَهْدِيّ The eschatological savior who fills the world with justice before the return of Isa . fol. 2v
  • II. Al-Masīḥ al-Dajjāl — The Deceiving Messiah الْمَسِيحُ الدَّجَّال A one-eyed figure who leads a kingdom of terror and tempts believers into idolatry. fol. 3r
  • III. Al-Jassāsa — The Spy الْجَسَّاسَة A fabulous, hairy female monster found on the island where the Dajjāl is chained. fol. 4v
  • IV. Jesus / ʿĪsā ibn Maryam عِيسَى ابْنُ مَرْيَمَ The Messiah who descends in Damascus, slays the Dajjāl, and unites the faithful. fol. 5r
  • V. Yaʾjūj wa-Maʾjūj — Gog and Magog يَأْجُوجُ وَمَأْجُوجُ Savage peoples unleashed after the Dajjāl's death to sow corruption before the final end. fol. 6v
  • VI. Al-Dābba — The Beast of the Earth الدَّابَّةُ مِنَ الْأَرْضِ A speaking beast that marks believers and disbelievers before the Hour. fol. 7r
  • VII. Isrāfīl — The Angel of the Trumpet إِسْرَافِيل The angel who blows the trumpet signalling the Hour and the resurrection of all souls. fol. 8v
